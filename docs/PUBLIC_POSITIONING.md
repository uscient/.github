# Public Positioning

## Primary Description

uScient is about user-owned knowledge and user-defined trust: making personal context, source authority, privacy, and information boundaries inspectable, adjustable, portable, and governed by the user.

## Short Description

uScient makes trust manageable around personal knowledge.

## AI Positioning

uScient is agent-compatible, not agent-run.

AI systems may help users inspect, label, summarize, redact, route, compare, draft, and audit context. They do not own knowledge, define trust policy, or become the authority over personal information.

Preferred language:

> uScient makes personal context usable by AI systems without surrendering authority to them.

Preferred language:

> uScient helps users define what AI systems may see, what they may do, and what boundaries they must respect.

Preferred language:

> Agents may operate within uScient boundaries. They do not set them.

## One-Line Options

Use these when space is limited:

- uScient is about user-owned knowledge and user-defined trust.
- uScient makes trust manageable around personal knowledge.
- uScient helps make context, source authority, and personal information boundaries inspectable and user-governed.
- uScient is agent-compatible, not agent-run.

## Public Emphasis

Public uScient language should emphasize:

- user agency
- personal knowledge control
- manageable trust
- source authority
- inspectable boundaries
- practical privacy/control
- evidence-based context handling
- developer trust through clear, inspectable work
- AI assistance governed by user-defined boundaries

## What To Avoid

Avoid describing uScient as:

- just privacy tools
- a finished platform
- an AI company
- a SaaS product
- an agent-run system
- a generic personal knowledge app
- a compliance product
- a security guarantee
- a vague trust layer

Avoid claims that imply:

- finished product maturity
- legal compliance
- complete privacy guarantees
- enterprise readiness
- patented technology
- commercial availability
- private roadmap commitments
- autonomous agent control over personal knowledge

## Tone

uScient should sound:

- precise
- calm
- serious
- technically credible
- user-centered
- restrained
- durable

uScient should not sound:

- breathless
- grandiose
- mystical
- generic
- defensive
- overmarketed
- like AI hype
- like agents are in charge

## Relationship To Tools

Tools are expressions of uScient doctrine.

They should demonstrate the principles:

- user-owned knowledge
- user-defined trust
- manageable trust
- source authority
- personal information control
- boundary-first AI assistance

Do not let a tool define the whole project.
